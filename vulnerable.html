VULNERABLE SUPPORT
Driven by compassion and the teachings of Islam, we introduce the Vulnerable Support project—a commitment to extending a helping hand to those facing hardships and vulnerabilities within our community.


In Islam, caring for the vulnerable, such as orphans, widows, and the economically disadvantaged, is not just a noble act but a duty. The Vulnerable Support project aims to provide essential assistance, including food, clothing, shelter, and medical care, to those who need it the most.


This initiative echoes the Quranic command:


"وَيُطۡعِمُونَ ٱلطَّعَامَ عَلَىٰ حُبِّهِۦ مِسۡكِينٗا وَيَتِيمٗا وَأَسِيرٗا"


"And they give food in spite of love for it to the needy, the orphan, and the captive..." (Quran 76:8)


Your support for the Vulnerable Support project is an embodiment of empathy and a demonstration of the principles of Islam. By contributing, you play a vital role in alleviating the struggles of those less fortunate and making a positive impact on their lives.


Join us in making a difference, as we stand united in our responsibility to uplift and care for the vulnerable members of our community.

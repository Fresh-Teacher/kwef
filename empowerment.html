COMMUNITY ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT
We are committed to tackling the underlying causes of poverty and social injustices to bring about lasting change in the lives of poor and vulnerable people. Our goal is to leave them better prepared to face the future with resilience and self-reliance.


Through a range of programs and initiatives, we aim to empower communities with the tools and resources they need to uplift themselves. By focusing on skill development, education, and sustainable income-generating projects, we work towards breaking the cycle of poverty and creating pathways towards economic independence.


We understand that true empowerment involves more than just providing financial aid. It's about nurturing individuals' potential, fostering entrepreneurship, and promoting self-sufficiency. Our collaborative efforts engage the community members, working together to identify their needs, strengths, and opportunities for growth.


Empowered individuals become the driving force behind positive change. They become agents of progress within their communities, sharing knowledge, skills, and innovative solutions to address challenges and create prosperity.


As we engage in this noble endeavor, let us remember the words of Allah in the Quran 2:177:

"لَيْسَ الْبِرَّ أَن تُوَلُّوا وُجُوهَكُمْ قِبَلَ الْمَشْرِقِ وَالْمَغْرِبِ وَلَٰكِنَّ الْبِرَّ مَنْ آمَنَ بِاللَّهِ وَالْيَوْمِ الْآخِرِ وَالْمَلَائِكَةِ وَالْكِتَابِ وَالنَّبِيِّينَ وَآتَى الْمَالَ عَلَىٰ حُبِّهِ ذَوِي الْقُرْبَىٰ وَالْيَتَامَىٰ وَالْمَسَاكِينَ وَابْنَ السَّبِيلِ وَالسَّائِلِينَ وَفِي الرِّقَابِ وَأَقَامَ الصَّلَاةَ وَآتَى الزَّكَاةَ وَالْمُوفُونَ بِعَهْدِهِمْ إِذَا عَاهَدُوا ۖ وَالصَّابِرِينَ فِي الْبَأْسَاءِ وَالضَّرَّاءِ وَحِينَ الْبَأْسِ ۗ أُولَٰئِكَ الَّذِينَ صَدَقُوا ۖ وَأُولَٰئِكَ هُمُ الْمُتَّقُونَ"


"Righteousness is not that you turn your faces toward the east or the west, but [true] righteousness is [in] one who believes in Allah, the Last Day, the Angels, the Book, and the Prophets and gives his wealth, in spite of love for it, to relatives, orphans, the needy, the traveler, those who ask [for help], and for freeing slaves; [and who] establishes prayer and gives Zakah; [those who] fulfill their promise when they promise; and [those who] are patient in poverty and hardship and during battle. Those are the ones who have been true, and it is those who are the righteous. "


Your support is crucial in building strong, let us join hands for self-reliant communities that can thrive and contribute to the overall well-being of society.

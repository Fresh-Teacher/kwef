CHILD PROTECTION INITIATIVE
Embracing the principles of compassion and safeguarding, we introduce the Child Protection Initiative—an essential endeavor aimed at ensuring the safety, well-being, and holistic development of our young ones.

In Islam, children are treasures entrusted to us by Allah. Through this initiative, we commit to creating a nurturing environment that shields them from harm, provides them with quality education, and nurtures their physical, emotional, and spiritual growth.

The Child Protection Initiative encompasses a range of activities, including educational workshops on child rights, awareness campaigns against child exploitation, and providing support to vulnerable children and their families.

This initiative is a reflection of the teachings of our beloved Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) who emphasized the care and protection of children.

"مَن لَّا يَرۡحَمۡنَا لَا يُرۡحَمۡ"

"Whoever does not show mercy to our young ones and honor to our elders is not one of us." (Sunan Ibn Majah)

Your support can make a significant difference in the lives of these children. By contributing to the Child Protection Initiative, you are helping us create a safe haven for our young ones to flourish, learn, and grow into responsible and empowered members of our community.

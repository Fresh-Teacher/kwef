Welcome to Kibale Welfare and Education Foundation


Welcome to the Kibale Welfare and Education Foundation Limited, a dedicated organization propelled by a profound commitment to uplift communities and instigate positive change. Our journey is guided by the unshakable principles of compassion, solidarity, and the teachings of Islam. Our goal is clear: to forge a lasting impact in the lives of those in need, as we tread the path of benevolence.

Our foundation is a tapestry woven with impactful projects, each thread addressing various dimensions of community well-being. From the formidable endeavor of constructing mosques to the creation of schools, establishment of medical centers, providing homes for the needy, and beyond, each project reflects our unrelenting drive to enrich education, health, and living conditions.

The Mosque Construction project, an emblem of faith, devotion, and unity, stands tall. These sacred havens are more than mere structures; they're sanctuaries of worship, contemplation, and spiritual ascension. They knit our community together, fostering an unbreakable sense of camaraderie.

Education, a beacon of empowerment, takes center stage in our efforts. The School Construction project is a testament to our belief in creating spaces of learning and growth. Education empowers individuals, transcending adversity, molding them into leaders and contributors to society.

Our Medical Centers extend a compassionate hand, ensuring access to vital healthcare for communities, thereby cultivating healthier lives.

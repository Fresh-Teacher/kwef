Description

KIBALE WELFARE & EDUCATION FOUNDATION LIMITED (KWEF-LTD) is an indigenous nonprofit organization based in Mid-Western Uganda region. With its head office in Kamwenge District, File No. MIA/NB/2022/10/5326 Plot 166 Kibale, Block 75, Nsorora cell, Kamwenge-Mbarara Road, Kamwenge District. KWEF has been in existence since 2015 operating in western region (Kamwenge, Kitagwenda, Kasese, Kyegegwa, Kabarole, Kyenjojo, Kazo, and Ibanda Districts).


Our Vision

To create a world where every human being attains their given rights to survival, protection, development, a healthy environment, and the feeling of belongingness.


Our Mission

To create a difference in the lives of Africa‚Äôs children, youth, orphans, widows, elders, disabled, and communities devastated by poverty, illiteracy, and diseases regardless of social, religious, economic, and political backgrounds to improve their quality of life.


Our Core Principles

  • Humanity: Dedicated to saving lives and ending poverty, we help people around the country at their most dire moments, save and protect their lives, alleviate suffering, and protect human dignity.

  • Inclusiveness: We work for the incorporation of needs and perspectives of people from diverse backgrounds and value the contributions of each and every human being.

  • Impartiality: We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, ethnicity, class, national origin, religion, gender, or political opinions.

  • Independence: We are autonomous and do not accept any political or ideological influence.

  • Voluntary Service: We are not profit-oriented. We aim to promote peace, understanding, cooperation, and solidarity through voluntary service.


Our Values

  • Sustainability: We work on developing sustainable solutions to address the most pressing needs of communities and leave a lasting impact on the lives of human beings.

  • Transparency: We ensure maintenance of openness and accountability in all our actions.

  • Innovation: We are dedicated to promoting novel and creative solutions which offer effective solutions to crises and vulnerabilities.

  • Partnership: We work together with partners to make fundamental changes in the living conditions of people in need and increase our capacity to address pressing human needs around the country.

Organisational Objectives

  1. Promote and improve access to quality education in vulnerable communities through construction of schools.
  2. Promote and improve access to health and hygiene in vulnerable communities through construction of hospitals and staging healthy camps.
  3. Improve the quality of life of vulnerable communities through construction of water sources and shelters for the vulnerable.
  4. Support orphans and vulnerable children through general welfare support, education, and health sponsorship.
  5. Poverty alleviation in marginalized communities through skills training and providing start-up kits to women and youth.
  6. Advocate for people living with HIV/AIDS, and fight against sexual abuses, early marriages, and gender-based violence.
  7. Put up income generating projects that will generate income to sustain the activities of the organization.
  8. Carry out any other activity that will be deemed important in achieving the organization's goals and objectives.

Sources of Funding

  • Funders, members, and well-wishers contributions in cash and in kind.
  • Community contributions mainly in terms of land and labour.
  • Donors from international relief organizations.
  • Agency funds from people who want charity work done on their behalf basing on the trust earned by the organization.
